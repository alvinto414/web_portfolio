Heuristic Evaluation

A detailed heuristic report on the Science World website.

Evaluation Date:

September 14, 2013

Responsibilities:

I was responsible for creating scenarios and tasks, evaluate different pages on the Science World website, as well as producing recommendations.

Outcome:

  • Able to evaluate websites against heuristic
  • Able to produce a detailed heuristic evaluation report
  • Report was later submitted to Science World

As a team, we performed a heuristic evaluation on the Science World's website to discover their usability problems. We first determined a list of user's goals so that we may use these cases investigate further. Once we have determined the lost of user's goal, then we analyze how these goals would be achieved by the users. We individually and as a team studied the tasks and determine the usability problems and assessed the severity of the problems. We then put our findings and suggested improvements into a report. This report was later submitted to Science World.
